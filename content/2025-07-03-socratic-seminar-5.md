- [Dust Expiry: Clean the UTXO set from spam](https://delvingbitcoin.org/t/dust-expiry-clean-the-utxo-set-from-spam/1707) -- RobinLinus proposa un soft-fork per eliminar el dust que no sera gastat del UTXO set.
